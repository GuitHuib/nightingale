Support Nightingale

As a 501(c)(3) not-for-profit, Nightingale Ensemble is dedicated to the expansion and growth of accessibility of all music in Chicago and across the country. We rely on your tax-deductible gifts to create new and exciting music, and open opportunities for ascending musicians, with a special emphasis on traditionally underserved commuities!

Join us by supporting Nightingale Ensemble’s mission of finding new and unique voices for collaborations that can be enjoyed by all.

We can also accept donations throught PayPal, Venmo(@Nightingale-Ensemble) or by physical check(mailto: 1343 W. Irving Park Road #13174 Chicago, IL 60613)
